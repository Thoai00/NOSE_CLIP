How It Works

Using our nasal clip is effortless and effective. Follow these steps to enjoy better airflow and relief from snoring or congestion.

Step 1 Clean

Clean your nose thoroughly to remove any dirt or oil. Let it dry completely before applying the tabs.

Step 2 Apply

Using the applicator, apply one adhesive tab to each side of your nose.

Step 3 Tab Placement

Ensure the steel disc is placed directly above the nostril and maintains full contact.

Step 4 Attach

Place the U-will band over your nose. The magnets will snap onto the tabs.
